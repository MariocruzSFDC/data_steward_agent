The "Cold Start" Solution

Reverse Engineering Structure

Problem: Onboarding is painful. SMBs and disorganized enterprises lack documentation for their naming conventions.

Solution: The Architect agent analyzes raw data strings without a rulebook, identifies patterns, and reconstructs the taxonomy rules automatically.

Strategic Value

  • Drastically lowers barrier to entry for new clients.
  • Automates the definition of "Data Standards".
  • Enables smaller customers to adopt enterprise-grade governance.

The "Trust" Engine

Automated Compliance Auditing

Problem: Human error in campaign naming destroys reporting accuracy. Clients like Telcel and Bloomberg cite this as a perennial challenge.

Solution: The Guardian agent ingests rules, scans data, categorizes errors (P0-P2), and auto-generates fixes.
